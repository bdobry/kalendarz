2. Bridge Day Highlighting (Problem 1)

OLD BEHAVIOR: Holidays on Tuesday or Thursday were highlighted as bridges.

NEW BEHAVIOR: The working day adjacent to the holiday is highlighted as a bridge day:

  • If holiday is on Tuesday, Monday is highlighted as bridge
  • If holiday is on Thursday, Friday is highlighted as bridge

Example: 2025 Bridge Days

  • May 1 (Thursday - Święto Pracy) → May 2 (Friday) is bridge
  • June 19 (Thursday - Boże Ciało) → June 20 (Friday) is bridge
  • November 11 (Tuesday - Narodowe Święto Niepodległości) → November 10 (Monday) is bridge
